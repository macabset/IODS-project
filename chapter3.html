This meand studying 5-10 hours weekly is associated with less high use than studying <2 hours. Category 4 (studying more than 10 hours weekly) was not significantly associated with high use. Male sex increased the probability of high use significantly. Family relations are not associated with high use. #Odds ratios and confidence intervals:

# define a loss function (mean prediction error)
loss_func <- function(class, prob) {
  n_wrong <- abs(class - prob) > 0.5
  mean(n_wrong)
}

# call loss_func to compute the average number of wrong predictions in the (training) data
loss_func(class = alc$high_use, prob = alc$probability)
